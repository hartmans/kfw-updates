Change Password

Before you start the process, create a strong password. It's best to start with a phrase instead of a word. Password Tips

To change your password:

  1. Click the Change Password button in the Home tab.
  2. Verfiy your username and enter your current password.
  3. Enter your new password twice. You must enter it the second time to make sure you have typed it correctly, since you cannot visually confirm what you have typed.
  4. Click Okay.

Remember to use your new password the next time you obtain tickets.
