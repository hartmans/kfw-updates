Destroy Tickets

Destroying your tickets when you no longer need them increases security. Once the tickets are destroyed they can no longer be used by anyone.
Note: Your tickets are not destroyed when you exit Leash unless you have selected Destroy Tickets on Exit in the Options menu.

To destroy all of the tickets on your local machine:

  1. Click the Destroy Ticket button.
  2. Click Okay to confirm that you want to destroy the tickets.

Before you access any of the services or computers that use your Kerberos password, you must get a new ticket.
