Get Tickets

Before you can access services that use Kerberos for security, you must obtain a Kerberos ticket.

To get a ticket:

  1. Click the Get Ticket button in the Home tab.
  2. Enter your user name if it is not already entered for you.
  3. Change the realm name if necessary.
  4. Enter your password.
  5. If you want to verify or change the ticket settings and they are hidden, click Show Advanced Settings at the bottom of the window and then make any necessary changes.
    Ticket settings help
  6. Click Okay.

Your new ticket is listed in the Leash window.
