Forget Saved Principals

Kerberos saves your principals for you if you select "Remember this principal" in the Get Ticket window. Saved principals then auto-complete when you start to type them and are available from a list in the Get Ticket and Change Password windows.

If you have saved a principal that you will no longer use, and you do not want that principal to auto-complete or appear in the saved principal list, tell Kerberos to forget your saved principals.

Note: When you select Forget Principals, Kerberos forgets all of your saved principals. It is not possible to forget some principals while remembering others.

Forget Saved Principals

  1. Find the More panel at the far right of the ribbon menu. If the panel options are not available, click the More button to show all options.
  2. Select Forget Principals.

All of your saved principals are removed from the saved list. Your tickets and your view of principals and tickets in the main window are unaffected.
